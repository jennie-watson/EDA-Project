Personal Finance 101

Congratulations on taking the first steps towards a better financial future! Personal Finance 101 is what I consider to be the first steps towards getting your financial life in order. This can look different to each person, whether this is making a realistic budget and sticking to it, setting up a high interest savings account, paying down debt fast or changing KiwiSaver providers. These are all important steps to take when managing your money.

Budgeting

Budgeting is a way of staying in control of your finances. Rather than wondering where your money has gone each payday, having an effective budget can help you keep on track with your spending and achieve your financial goals sooner. I recommend using the Sorted Budgeting Tool> to get you started.
